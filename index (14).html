01 — About

Where Minds Converge

Techfest is the annual Science & Technology festival of IIT Bombay — the most prestigious engineering institution in Asia. Since 1998, it has grown into a phenomenon that attracts the sharpest technical minds from across the world.

From combat robotics to quantum computing challenges, Nobel Prize lecture series to international drone racing — Techfest is where the impossible is engineered.

This year marks the 30th edition — the Neural Interface Edition. A celebration of human augmentation, artificial intelligence, and the beautiful collision between carbon and silicon.
